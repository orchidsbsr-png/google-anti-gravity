===========================================
PRODUCT IMAGE NAMING GUIDE
===========================================

Upload your product images to this folder with names matching your products.

SUPPORTED FILE FORMATS:
- .jpg / .jpeg
- .png
- .webp
- .gif

EXACT PRODUCT NAMES (use these exactly):
===========================================
1. Red Apples
   → Name your file: "Red Apples.jpg" (or .png, .webp, etc.)

2. Orange Persimmons
   → Name your file: "Orange Persimmons.jpg"

3. Fuzzy Kiwis
   → Name your file: "Fuzzy Kiwis.jpg"

4. Peaches
   → Name your file: "Peaches.jpg"

5. Plums
   → Name your file: "Plums.jpg"

6. Premium Asian Pear (or Pears)
   → Name your file: "Premium Asian Pear.jpg" or "Pears.jpg"

FLEXIBLE NAMING:
===========================================
The app will also recognize these variations:
- Spaces: "Red Apples.jpg"
- Underscores: "Red_Apples.jpg"
- Hyphens: "Red-Apples.jpg"
- Lowercase: "red apples.jpg", "red_apples.jpg", "red-apples.jpg"

EXAMPLES:
===========================================
✅ Good names:
   - Red Apples.jpg
   - Peaches.png
   - Fuzzy Kiwis.webp
   - Orange_Persimmons.jpg
   - plums.jpg

❌ Bad names:
   - apple.jpg (missing "Red")
   - fruit1.jpg (not matching product name)
   - IMG_1234.jpg (not descriptive)

QUICK START:
===========================================
1. Take photos of your fruits or download product images
2. Name them exactly as your products (see list above)
3. Place them in this folder
4. Restart your dev server (npm run dev)
5. The app will automatically use your images!

NOTE: If an image is not found, the app will use online placeholder images as fallback.
