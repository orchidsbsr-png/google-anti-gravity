===========================================
LANDSCAPE IMAGE GUIDE
===========================================

Upload your landscape/mountain/valley image to this folder.

IMAGE NAMING:
===========================================
Name your landscape image as one of these:
- "landscape.jpg" (preferred)
- "Landscape.jpg"
- "landscape.png"
- "mountain.jpg"
- "valley.jpg"

IMAGE REQUIREMENTS:
===========================================
- Landscape orientation (wider than tall)
- High resolution (at least 1920x1080 for best quality)
- JPG or PNG format
- The image will be used as a full-width background
- It will be cropped/positioned to center

The app will automatically:
- Use the image as a full-width hero background
- Apply a smooth green overlay for text readability
- Add smooth fade-in and slide-up animations
- Make it responsive for all screen sizes

SUPPORTED FORMATS:
- .jpg / .jpeg
- .png

QUICK START:
===========================================
1. Take or download a beautiful landscape photo of mountains/valleys
2. Name it "landscape.jpg"
3. Place it in this folder (public/images/products/)
4. The app will automatically use it on the landing page!

NOTE: The image will have a subtle green overlay to ensure text remains readable.
