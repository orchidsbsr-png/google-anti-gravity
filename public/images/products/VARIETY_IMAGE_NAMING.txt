IMAGE CROPPING:
===========================================
All images will be automatically cropped to center and cover.
Make sure your images are:
- Square or close to square (1:1 ratio) for best results
- High quality (at least 400x400 pixels)
- Well-lit and clear

The app uses object-fit: cover with object-position: center
to ensure all images display evenly, cropping excess if needed.

SUPPORTED FORMATS:
- .jpg / .jpeg
- .png
- .webp
- .gif

QUICK START:
===========================================
1. Take photos of each variety or download product images
2. Name them exactly as shown above
3. Place them in this folder (public/images/products/)
4. The app will automatically detect and use them!

NOTE: If an image is not found, the app will use online placeholder images as fallback.
